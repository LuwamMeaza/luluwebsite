ABOUT US

ERI HABESHA TRADITIONAL MISSION IS TO PROVIDE WOMEN AND MEN WITH THE MOST LUXURIOUS TRADITIONAL AND MODERN EAST AFRICA CLOSES APPAREL ON THE MARKET.

Our products are unique and fashionable designs, but more importantly practical and technical. Designed by all top designers from Eritrea and Ethiopia, our apparel is crafted for women and men. Our clothes are one of the top designs in the market.

GREEN LIVING / MANUFACTURING

We are also committed to our environment and our community by continually striving to make our manufacturing facility greener each day by recycling plant waste and by using earth friendly materials in our products. We follow fair labor practices with all of our employees and business partners to create a sustainable work environment.
